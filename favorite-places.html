Kangaroo Island

Yes, I know what you're thinking, "an island full of kangaroos?" Kind of, but not really. Kangaroo island lies just Southwest from the tip of Adelaide Australia. What makes this island so fasinating is it's diversity in regards to terrian and animal life. To give you a quick idea of what I'm talking about, if you only had a short period of time to travel, but you wanted to get a great idea of the terrian, animals and feel of most of Australia, you can experience that on Kangaroo Island. Yes the island does have a lot of whild kangaroo, but you'll see wild wallabies, qualabears, sea lions, all kinds mammals, birds, reptiles/aphebians and insects. I is truley an adventure. Did I mentioned terrian?! From sand dunes, to thick brush, on to gorgious beach and more. This place has room for a kinds of active activities which include:

  • Hiking
  • Sand Boarding
  • Surfing
  • Animal Watching
  • Off Roading

These are just to name a few. The island also has all kinds of unique places to eat and enjoy the local wines and beers. If you have a little extra cash to spare, I'd suggest you pay for a tour package, where a local will pick you up in Adelaide and take you all around the island explaing all the history and animal life. You will get a chance to spend the night under the stars as you share campfire stories and cook shrimp on the barbie. I can sit here all day explaing the details of this little oasis, but if you want more information, view more on it on one of my favorite websites, Loney Planet.

I hope you enjoyed my brief breakdown of Kangaroo Island. Happy travels, and as the locals say, "Gu day mate."
